## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="What you'll need">
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for registration
- No software installation required – everything runs in your browser
</Callout>

Before starting, ensure you have access to `https://app.sim1.com`, the main Sim1 dashboard.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register and get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the Sim1 registration page at `https://app.sim1.com/register`.

    Click **Register** and fill in your details:
    
    | Field       | Example Value              |
    |-------------|----------------------------|
    | Email       | user@example.com           |
    | Password    | A strong password (8+ chars)|
    | Username    | yourusername               |
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Sim1.
    
    Click the verification link to activate your account.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Didn't receive the email? Check your spam folder or request a resend from the login page.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Access Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In" icon="log-in">
    Go to `https://app.sim1.com/login`.
    
    Enter your email and password, then click **Anmelden** (Sign In).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Once logged in, you'll see the main dashboard with:
    
    - Upcoming matches
    - Active tip rounds
    - Your predictions history
    
    Use the navigation menu to browse leagues like Bundesliga or Premier League.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Join an Existing Tip Round

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search and Join" icon="search">
    1. On the dashboard, click **Tipptreffpunkte** (Tip Rounds).
    2. Search for public rounds by league or keyword, e.g., "Bundesliga".
    3. Select a round and click **Beitreten** (Join).
    
    You're now ready to predict!
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Join by Invite Code" icon="key">
    Ask the organizer for an invite code.
    
    1. Go to **Join Round** on dashboard.
    2. Enter the code and confirm.
    
    <Image
      src="https://via.placeholder.com/600x300?text=Join+Round+Screen"
      alt="Sim1 join round interface"
      width="600"
      height="300"
    />
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Make Your First Prediction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Match" icon="sports-soccer">
    In your joined tip round, pick an upcoming match, e.g., Bayern Munich vs. Dortmund.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Prediction" icon="check-circle">
    Choose scores or outcomes:
    
    - Home: `3`
    - Away: `1`
    
    Click **Tipp abgeben** (Submit Tip).
    
    <Callout kind="success">
      Success! Your prediction is saved. Track results in real-time.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>
