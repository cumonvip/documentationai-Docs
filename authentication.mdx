## Overview

Sim1 provides secure authentication for managing your sports prediction tips and account data. You can register, log in via web or API, recover passwords, and update preferences while following best practices to protect your information.

<Callout kind="alert">
Always use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available to safeguard your tips and personal data.
</Callout>

## Register a New Account

Create your Sim1 account to start participating in tip prediction rounds for leagues like Bundesliga or Premier League.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out the Form" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your email, create a strong password, and provide a display name for tip communities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Sim1 and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup" icon="check-circle">
    Log in and set your initial preferences, such as favorite leagues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In to Your Account

Access Sim1 through the web dashboard or integrate via API for automated tip submissions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Login" icon="globe">
    Go to `https://dashboard.example.com/login`, enter your credentials, and submit.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Login" icon="code">
    Use the authentication endpoint to obtain a session token.

    <Request tabs="JavaScript,cURL" show-lines="true">
      ```javascript
      const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/auth/login', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: { 'Content-Type': 'application/json' },
        body: JSON.stringify({
          email: 'user@example.com',
          password: 'YOUR_PASSWORD'
        })
      });
      const data = await response.json();
      console.log(data.token);
      ```
      ```bash
      curl -X POST https://api.example.com/auth/login \
        -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
        -d '{"email":"user@example.com","password":"YOUR_PASSWORD"}'
      ```
    </Request>

    <ParamField header="Authorization" param-type="string" required="true">
      Bearer token obtained after login: `Bearer YOUR_TOKEN`.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField body="email" param-type="string" required="true">
      Your registered email address.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField body="password" param-type="string" required="true">
      Your account password.
    </ParamField>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Password Recovery and Reset

Forgot your password? Follow these steps to regain access securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Reset" icon="key">
    On the login page, click `Forgot Password?` and enter your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email" icon="mail">
    Sim1 sends a reset link valid for 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password" icon="lock">
    Click the link, enter a new strong password, and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Use your new credentials to access your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Updating Account Details

Manage your profile, preferences, and security settings from the dashboard.

<Expandable title="How to Update Profile" default-open="true">
  1. Log in to `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  2. Navigate to **Account Settings**.
  3. Edit email, name, or password.
  4. Save changes and verify if prompted.

  For API updates:

  ```javascript
  await fetch('https://api.example.com/account', {
    method: 'PATCH',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_TOKEN',
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({ displayName: 'New Name' })
  });
  ```
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Enable Two-Factor Authentication">
  In **Security Settings**, generate a TOTP secret and scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
</Expandable>

## Security Best Practices

Protect your Sim1 account and tip data with these tips.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strong Passwords" icon="lock" horizontal>
    Use at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid reusing passwords across sites.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enable 2FA" icon="shield" horizontal>
    Activate two-factor authentication to add an extra layer beyond passwords.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure API Tokens" icon="key" horizontal>
    Regenerate tokens regularly and store them securely—never in client-side code.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monitor Activity" icon="eye" horizontal>
    Review login history in your dashboard and report suspicious activity immediately.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<ResponseField name="token" field-type="string" required="true">
  JWT token for authenticated requests, expires in 24 hours.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="user" field-type="object">
  Contains `id`, `email`, and `displayName`.
</ResponseField>
