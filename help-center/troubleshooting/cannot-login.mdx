---
title: Cannot Log In
description: Steps to fix login issues in Sim1.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page keeps refreshing
- Wrong password error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email and password">
    Make sure both are correct and check for typos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen and follow the link in your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Cached data can block login; clear cookies then try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
