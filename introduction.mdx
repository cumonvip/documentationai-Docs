## Overview

Sim1 lets you create and join exciting sports prediction tip rounds for leagues like the Bundesliga, Premier League, cups, and world championships. You can jump into existing tip communities or build your own to challenge friends, colleagues, or partners—all for free. Whether you're a casual fan or organizing group competitions, Sim1 makes predicting match outcomes fun and social.

This platform handles everything from registration to real-time scoring, so you focus on the thrill of the game.
